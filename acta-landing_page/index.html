our mission:

To increase participation in local governments by leveraging technology. Through our digital platform, we deliver to citizens all the relevant information on issues that impact their lives in an easily-digestible, personalized format straight to their digital "door-step". We hope to empower citizens with relevant and actionable information, while providing policy makers with real-time community input.

Our platform empowers local governments to equitably:

Create Engaging Content

We understand government employees have a lot on their plate. Our automated platform cuts down workflows and saves time! Simply fill out a form each week and our platform will automatically format, create, and send all the emails to citizens!

Communicate with Citizens

We believe in the adage of meeting people where they are. We are the only platform that delivers information directly to each resident's digital "door-step" and personalize the information to maximize engagement. We help local governments significantly increase reach at a fraction of the cost.

Close the Feedback Loop

With our data, we show local governments which communities they are reaching and which demographics they have yet to reach. From this information, municipalities can further understand how various groups of citizens in their city are reacting to policy, zoning laws, regulations, etc. Our goals are to empower local government to engage all of their constituents, remove the barriers of participation for communities, and foster a healthier civic life for all.

Inform Decisions

Our data dashboard provides actionable insights to inform local government decision-makers. We visualize all feedback data in an easy-to-read format and provide overall summary data and sentiment analysis. Our platform also allows our government partners to drill down and segment data by demographics, policy, location, and any combination of those.
